UNREGULATED: How HOAs Threaten the American Dream is a documentary that explores the power dynamics of Homeowners' Associations and their impact on American families, highlighting real stories and advocating for legislative change. This film is currently in post-production.

Help us get this film across the finish line by donating and by sharing this website with HOA advocates, groups, legislators, and others interested.

The Story

The Scope of the Problem

Roughly 74 million Americans live in communities governed by Homeowners' Associations. New developments are giving rise to expanding this if trends continue for future homeowners. Many do not understand the impact HOAs have on their communities, families, and the American dream.

Meet Kenneth Spresley

This exposé follows Kenneth Spresley, a Texas homeowner and United States Air Force veteran as he seeks to understand HOAs, their origins, their purpose, and the power they possess.

Learn More

Why This Matters

HOAs wield significant power over homeowners' property rights, financial obligations, and quality of life. Understanding this power dynamic is essential for protecting the American dream and ensuring fair governance for millions of families.
