About WCAG 2.1 Compliance

Level A: Basic accessibility. The minimum standard for web accessibility.

Level AA: Enhanced accessibility. Covers the needs of most users, recommended for compliance.

Level AAA: Advanced accessibility. Maximum level, benefits specialized users.

This checklist focuses on WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
